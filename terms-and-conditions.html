8. Dukanoh Safe Checkout

Dukanoh Safe Checkout is a discretionary purchase resolution scheme available on eligible Transactions. It is not a contract of insurance and is not regulated as such. It is a voluntary scheme operated by Dukanoh to help resolve transactional disputes between Buyers and Sellers.

Dukanoh Safe Checkout applies to Transactions with an item value of up to £1,000. Transactions above this value are not covered by Dukanoh Safe Checkout, though your statutory rights remain unaffected.

8.1 What is Covered

Dukanoh will use reasonable endeavours to facilitate a refund of the original purchase price (up to £1,000) where:

  • Your item is not delivered within a reasonable period and cannot be traced.
  • The item received is significantly not as described — materially different in condition, specification, or authenticity from the Listing.
  • The item received is counterfeit.
  • The item arrives damaged due to inadequate packaging by the Seller.

8.2 How Claims are Funded

Where a Dukanoh Safe Checkout claim is upheld, Dukanoh will seek to fund the refund through the following in order of priority:

  • Seller recovery — from funds held by Stripe that have not yet been released to the Seller.
  • Stripe dispute mechanisms — where Stripe’s payment dispute or chargeback process applies.
  • Dukanoh discretion — in limited circumstances, Dukanoh may at its sole discretion contribute toward a refund where recovery through the above channels has been exhausted.

Where funds have been automatically released to a Seller prior to a dispute being raised, Dukanoh will use reasonable efforts to recover those funds but cannot guarantee recovery. In such cases your recourse may be against the Seller directly or via your card issuer’s chargeback process.

8.3 How to Claim

  • Raise a dispute via the in-app “Report an Issue” button within 48 hours of the delivery being marked as delivered.
  • Provide photographic or other evidence of the issue.
  • Dukanoh’s Trust & Safety team will review the claim and respond within 7 days.
  • If the claim is upheld, a refund will be processed within 5–10 business days to your original payment method, subject to successful recovery under clause 8.2.

8.4 Exclusions

Dukanoh Safe Checkout does not apply where:

  • The Transaction value exceeds £1,000.
  • The dispute is raised after the 48-hour window.
  • The item has been used, altered, or damaged after receipt by the Buyer.
  • The Buyer has confirmed receipt before raising the dispute.
  • The issue is a change of mind rather than a material discrepancy.
  • The Buyer attempted to complete the transaction off-platform.
  • The item was listed in a category explicitly excluded from Dukanoh Safe Checkout (see www.dukanoh.com/safe-checkout for exclusion list).
